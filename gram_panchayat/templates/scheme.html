📜 Gram Panchayat Schemes

🏠 Rural Housing Scheme

About: The Rural Housing Scheme provides financial support to poor families for constructing safe and permanent houses in villages.

Eligibility: Families below poverty line (BPL) or without permanent housing.

Benefits: Subsidy amount up to ₹1.2 – ₹1.5 lakh (varies by state).

Documents Required: Aadhaar card, ration card, income proof, land ownership proof.

How to Apply: Visit Gram Panchayat office → Submit application form → Verification by officials → Approval & fund release.

🚰 Clean Water Mission

About: Ensures safe drinking water supply and sanitation facilities for all households.

Eligibility: All rural households without piped drinking water connection.

Benefits: Free water tap connection, improved sanitation, regular water supply.

Documents Required: Aadhaar card, residence proof, village certificate.

How to Apply: Apply at Panchayat office → Field inspection → Water connection installation.

👷 MGNREGA Employment

About: Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA) guarantees 100 days of wage employment to rural households.

Eligibility: Any adult member of a rural household willing to do unskilled manual work.

Benefits: 100 days guaranteed wage employment, equal wages for men & women, local work availability.

Documents Required: Aadhaar card, job card (issued under MGNREGA), bank account details.

How to Apply: Apply at Gram Panchayat → Get job card → Demand for work → Panchayat assigns work within 15 days.
